# Google Search and Activity Settings

This document outlines how Google uses your activity to personalize your experience.

## Activity

When you save history to your account as part of your Web & App Activity, Google can use it to personalize your experience.

### Search History
- **Status**: Saving

## Content Management

You can manage the kinds of search results you see by setting preferences for personalization and explicit content.

### Search Personalization
- **Status**: On

### SafeSearch
- **Status**: Filtering on

# Startup Actionable Steps

1.  **Identify & solve real user problems**—seek direct feedback from early adopters.
2.  **Focus on building a simple MVP (Minimum Viable Product)** and iterate fast.
3.  **Track key metrics**: user retention, engagement, and monetization.
4.  **Ensure smooth onboarding and clear product value proposition (Xeref)**.
5.  **Leverage automation (AI/bots)** for operational efficiency and unique features.
6.  **Build a strong online presence** and regularly share progress/content.
7.  **Network with industry peers** and seek relevant mentors.
8.  **Optimize productivity with disciplined sprints** (like your 90-min approach).
9.  **Regularly review feedback, adapt roadmap, and declutter features**.
10. **Protect your time**—limit distractions and focus on what drives growth.
